How to Use

1. Live Session (Daily Tracking)
Fill in the session metadata (Faculty, Subject, Class, etc.) at the top.

Drag and drop your raw student list (Excel, PDF, or Word).

Mark attendance using the P and A buttons. Use the Auto-Absent button to quickly finish the list.

Export the daily session as an Excel sheet or a clean PDF.

2. Merge Reports (Master & Semester)
Switch to the Merge Reports tab.

To build a Master Report: Drop in your generated daily Excel files. Click Generate Master PDF.

To build a Semester Report: Drop in your previously generated Master PDFs. Click Generate Semester PDF. The system will scan the Master sheets, crunch the attended/total math, and spit out the final semester ledger.
